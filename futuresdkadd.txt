Based on your schema, which supports subscriptions/payments, user notifications, creator earnings, and content views, here are the most relevant and powerful SDKs you can integrate into your stack (React Native frontend + Node.js/Prisma backend):

1. Payments & Subscriptions (for planId and payments)
Stripe SDK:
Frontend: @stripe/stripe-react-native
Backend: stripe (Node.js SDK)
Why: Best for processing credit card payments directly in-app, setting up recurring billing/subscriptions for your plans, and issuing payouts to creators for their earnings.
RevenueCat SDK:
Frontend: react-native-purchases
Why: Simplifies managing Apple App Store & Google Play Store In-App Purchases (IAP) and subscription status, mapping perfectly to your plan database models.
2. Push Notifications (for notifications)
Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM):
Frontend: @react-native-firebase/app & @react-native-firebase/messaging
Backend: firebase-admin
Why: Essential for sending real-time transactional push notifications (e.g., when a user gets a new follower, a post is liked, or a challenge is completed).
OneSignal SDK:
Frontend: react-native-onesignal
Why: A premium alternative to pure FCM that provides a rich dashboard, audience segmentation, and built-in email/SMS templates out of the box.
3. Image Uploads & CDN Storage (for profilePicture & Post Banners)
AWS S3 SDK:
Backend: @aws-sdk/client-s3
Why: Secure cloud storage for user avatars and post images. You can use React Native to upload directly to S3 via pre-signed URLs generated by your Prisma backend.
Cloudinary SDK:
Frontend: cloudinary-react-native
Why: Provides automated image optimization, resizing, and responsive CDN delivery so images load instantly on mobile devices.
4. Crash Reporting & Observability
Sentry SDK:
Frontend: @sentry/react-native
Backend: @sentry/node
Why: Vital for monitoring app health. It captures crashes and JavaScript exceptions in production, showing you the exact file and line number where the issue occurred.
Firebase Crashlytics:
Frontend: @react-native-firebase/crashlytics
Why: Highly integrated, lightweight native crash reporter that group issues by frequency and OS versions.
5. Analytics & Engagement Tracking (for viewedPosts and challenges)
Mixpanel or Amplitude SDK:
Frontend: @mixpanel/mixpanel-react-native or @amplitude/analytics-react-native
Why: Unlike basic Google Analytics, these allow you to track user-specific funnels, retention, and exact actions (like reading a post or upgrading a plan) to analyze engagement.
